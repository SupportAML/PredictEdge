# Prediction Market Analytics: What Every Serious Trader Needs in 2026

Prediction markets crossed **$200 billion in annualized volume** in 2026. Kalshi alone is valued at $22 billion. Polymarket hit $8 billion in monthly volume. DraftKings launched predictions in 38 states.

Yet the analytics tooling looks like crypto in 2017 — fragmented, incomplete, and mostly built for one platform.

We catalogued **170+ prediction market tools**. Here's what we found:

## The Current Landscape

| Tool | What It Does | What It Doesn't |
|---|---|---|
| **Verso** | Bloomberg-style terminal | Too expensive for retail traders |

| **TREMOR** | SQL analytics, 140K+ markets | Developer-oriented, not consumer-friendly |
| **Polysights** | 30+ custom metrics | Polymarket only |
| **kalshi-dash** | Open-source P&L tracker | CSV upload only, no real-time data |
| **ArbBets** | AI arbitrage detection | Single feature, no portfolio view |

Notice a pattern? **90% of tools are Polymarket-first.** Kalshi and DraftKings traders are massively underserved.

And not a single tool combines portfolio tracking + tax reporting + alerts + cross-platform analytics in one place.

## What Active Traders Actually Need

Based on analyzing Trustpilot reviews (Kalshi: 1.9/5 stars), BBB complaints (153 in 3 years), Reddit threads, and App Store reviews, here are the top unmet needs:

### 1. Real Portfolio Analytics

Not just a P&L number — max drawdown, Sharpe ratio, win rate by category, return distributions. The kind of analytics stock traders have had for 20 years.

### 2. Tax-Ready Reporting

Kalshi doesn't issue 1099-Bs. Traders spend hours rebuilding their tax records from CSV exports. CPAs charge $500+ for manual reconstruction.

### 3. Conditional Alerts

Kalshi's native alerts are basic push notifications. Traders want price-level triggers ("alert me when contract X hits $0.45"), volume spike detection, and new market notifications.

### 4. Cross-Platform View

With Kalshi, Polymarket, and DraftKings all offering overlapping markets, traders need to compare odds and find best execution. The neutral aggregator (Dome) was acquired by Polymarket — no independent option remains.

### 5. AI-Powered Value Detection

Compare market odds to model-derived probabilities. Find the +EV bets before the crowd.
